Paropakar Sanstha

Paropakar Sanstha is a caring organization dedicated to supporting elderly and needy people with dignity, comfort, and compassion. Our mission is to create a safe and respectful environment where every individual feels valued and protected.

We focus on providing essential services such as shelter, food, medical care, emotional support, and daily assistance for those in need. Our team works with dedication to improve the quality of life of the people we serve.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate care and support for the elderly and vulnerable members of society.

Our Vision

To build a community where every senior citizen lives with dignity, safety, and care.
