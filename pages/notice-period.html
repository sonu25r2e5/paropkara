Important Notice Period Information

This page shares the notice period guidelines for visitors, staff, volunteers, and service requests at Paropakar Sanstha.

General Notice Please inform us at least 7 days in advance for planned visits or changes.
Staff Notice Staff members should provide a 30-day notice before leaving their role.
Emergency Cases Emergency support can be requested immediately by contacting the office.

Guidelines

  • Submit written notice for leave, role changes, or service changes whenever possible.
  • Visitors should confirm their visit date and time before arriving.
  • Volunteers should inform the coordinator if they cannot attend scheduled work.
  • For urgent matters, contact the organization directly by phone.

Need help? Please contact Paropakar Sanstha for questions about notice periods, visits, volunteering, or care service updates.
