Services

Paropakar Sanstha and Anusandhan Kendra provides comprehensive care and support services for senior citizens to promote their health, safety, comfort, and overall well-being. The organization offers residential care along with medical, nursing, and rehabilitative services delivered by trained professionals. It also focuses on improving the quality of life of older adults by meeting their physical, emotional, and social needs in a supportive environment.

  • 24-hour residential care and supervision
  • Medical and nursing care
  • 24-hour residential care and supervision
  • Assistance with activities of daily living (bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility)
  • Oxygen therapy and other supportive medical services
  • Counseling and emotional support
  • Temporary, permanent, and day-care services for senior citizens
