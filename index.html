Paropakar Tatha Anushandhan Kendra

We provide care, shelter, medical support, and compassionate services for senior citizens and vulnerable people. Our mission is to create a safe and respectful home for those who need support and dignity.

Our Services

Cooking

We prepare fresh and hygienic food every day with care, keeping the health and comfort of elderly people in mind.

Medical Care

We support residents with regular health checkups, basic medical attention, and timely care whenever they need help.

Meal and Nutrition Support

Nutritious meals are planned to support strength, wellness, and daily energy for senior citizens and vulnerable people.
